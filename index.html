Project For Internalising Externalities Voluntarily

We know flights are cheaper than they should be. We know that airline companies are passing costs onto society and the environment in search of profits. We believe people want to face up to the truth about the costs of their actions.

We want to offer a way for people to pay the full amount for their flights and join a community putting those extra costs towards projects that benefit the world.

Our project aims to provide a solution for individuals who are concerned about the environmental impact of their flights. With our project, people have the opportunity to voluntarily donate an amount that reflects the true cost of their flight. By doing so, they contribute to internalizing the externalities associated with air travel.

The purpose of this project is not only to encourage individuals to take responsibility for their actions but also to showcase to decision makers the strong desire of people to contribute towards mitigating the environmental impact of air travel. While multinational corporations may not prioritize environmental concerns, our platform empowers individuals to show their commitment and care.

When individuals make a donation, they are automatically granted the right to participate in a citizen's assembly. This assembly convenes every three months, allowing participants to have a vote on how the donated money is utilized. This democratic process ensures that the funds are allocated according to the collective decisions and priorities of the participants.

By combining the concept of voluntary donations and citizen engagement, our platform promotes sustainable practices and empowers individuals to actively contribute to mitigating the environmental impact of air travel.
